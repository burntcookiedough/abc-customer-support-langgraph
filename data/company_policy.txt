ABC Technologies Customer Policy Document

Refund Policy:
Refund requests for annual subscriptions must be reviewed by a human supervisor. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7 to 10 business days.

Cancellation Policy:
Subscription cancellation requests must be reviewed before final confirmation. Customers may continue using the product until the end of the paid billing period unless otherwise approved.

Account Closure:
Account closure requests require human approval because they may permanently remove access to workspaces, users, and business records.

Compensation and Management Escalation:
Compensation requests and escalation to management must be reviewed by a supervisor before a final commitment is sent to the customer.
