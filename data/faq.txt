ABC Technologies FAQ

Password Reset:
Customers can reset passwords from the login page by selecting "Forgot password" and following the secure link sent by email.

Invoices:
Invoices are available from Billing Settings. Account owners and billing administrators can download invoice copies.

Profile Updates:
Users can update profile details from Account Settings. Some account-level changes require administrator permissions.

Support Hours:
Standard support is available on business days. Enterprise customers may have enhanced support terms.
